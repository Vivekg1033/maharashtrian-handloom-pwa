Our Heritage Story

Founded in 1998, Maharashtrian Handloom & Textiles began with a mission to preserve and promote the rich textile heritage of Maharashtra. We work directly with skilled artisans across the state to bring authentic handcrafted textiles to a global audience while ensuring fair compensation and sustainable practices.

Each piece in our collection represents centuries of cultural tradition, with designs and techniques that have been passed down through generations. We're proud to play a role in keeping these art forms alive and creating economic opportunities for artisan communities.
